Purpose

At Atlanta FoodQuest, our mission is to connect food enthusiasts with the diverse culinary delights of the greater Atlanta area. We aim to simplify the search for the perfect place to eat by providing a comprehensive, user-friendly platform that highlights the best restaurants and eateries. Through our app, we strive to celebrate Atlanta’s rich food culture and foster a community of passionate food lovers who explore, discover, and share their dining experiences. Whether you’re craving a classic Southern dish or new fusion cuisine, ATL FoodQuest is here to guide you to your next great find.

Features

Learn what benefits you can gain by using this site.

Ease of Access

We give users the ability to explore the map of restaurants without making an account. Find quick ratings and filters to gain the most information in the least amount of time.

Quick Results

Use an extensive array of filters and features to quickly narrow down the type of restaurant the user may want to explore. Gain access to a randomly generated list of restaurants, all with the click of a button.

User Benefits

Registered Users gain access to more in depth features on the restaurants they may want to explore. Mark favorites, read reviews, and even leave your own once you become a registered user.
